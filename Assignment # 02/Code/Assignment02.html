Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Use W, A, S, and D to move around the scene. R and F are used to rotate the camera vertically. You can use Q and E to rotate camera on its origin.
You can manually change planet and user control parameters from the contols tab on top right corner of the screen.

The simulation has been designed taking into consideration the original attributes of planets like their size, distance from sun, and period of rotation around their own axis as well as around the sun. To find more about these planets, visit the link: Planets
